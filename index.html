ü™ì WOODCUTTING SYSTEM

Woodcutting is essential for construction, crafting, and fuel. Use /cuttree near marked trees.

Tree Types:

Small Trees - White branch variety (Direct to inventory)
Big Trees - Requires DFT truck transport
üì¶
After cutting big trees, wood stacks in hands. Press N to load wood onto nearby DFT trucks.

Transportation & Storage:

DFT Truck Locations: Lumber Camp, Fort Carson, Faction Bases

Warehouse Storage: Drive loaded trucks to warehouses. Park and press N to store wood.

üè≠ WAREHOUSE SYSTEM

Wood storage depends on your faction and faction tier clearance level.

Blue Warehouse - Clearance level 0
Red Warehouse - Clearance level 1
/warehouse storage
View contents and withdraw items
/warehouse store
Store items in warehouse
‚ö†Ô∏è ACCESS REQUIREMENTS

Each warehouse displays its required clearance level. You must meet or exceed this requirement to access.

üìã SIDE QUEST SYSTEM

Side quests are secondary tasks given by NPCs (Actors) found on islands outside San Andreas. Each task offers unique rewards including items, money, and furniture materials.

Side Quest NPC Actor Example

Quest Commands:

/takesidequest
Take a side quest from an actor (ONE active quest at a time)
/cancelsidequest
Cancel your current side quest
/completesidequest
Complete current side quest when requirements met
‚ö†Ô∏è QUEST LIMITATION

You can only have ONE active side quest at a time. Some quest items are needed for other questlines - plan carefully!
