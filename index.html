ü™ì WOODCUTTING SYSTEM 2

Woodcutting is essential for construction, crafting, and fuel. Use /cuttree near marked trees.

Tree Types:

Small Trees - White branch variety (Direct to inventory)
Big Trees - Requires DFT truck transport
üì¶
After cutting big trees, wood stacks in hands. Press N to load wood onto nearby DFT trucks.

Transportation & Storage:

DFT Truck Locations: Lumber Camp, Fort Carson, Faction Bases

Warehouse Storage: Drive loaded trucks to warehouses. Park and press N to store wood.
