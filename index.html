Azure Blob Storage Music

This website uses Azure Blob Storage for permanent MP3 streaming in SA:MP.

How to Use:

  1. Go to Songs tab to browse music
  2. Click "Copy URL" on any song
  3. In SA:MP, type: /stream [URL]
  4. Music plays instantly in-game!
